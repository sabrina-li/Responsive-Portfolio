Full-stack developer based in Atlanta, Georgia. I'm a professionally trained developer with a background in enterprise applications, hold certification in machine learning and full-stack bootcamp from Georgia Tech. I have a passion for technology that is changing people's lives for the better. People spend hours on websites and mobile applications makes me believe that well designed, professionally developed websites are the true game changers. I am flexible, a quick learner, and enjoy developing websites and apps that are fun, innovative and make people’s day a little better. I’ve even worked as a consultant for my clients who are also developers. I’m proficient in languages such as JavaScript, CSS, HTML, and Node.js, with experience in C++, Java, React, and SQL/NoSQL. Besides my passion for the tech world, I love the great outdoors and my design inspiration comes from the harmony and ecosystem in nature. So let's work together to make this world a better place.
